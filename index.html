Red Octopus Solutions

Red Octopus Solutions supports organisations with technology-enabled learning, capability development and knowledge-sharing approaches designed to improve practice in real working environments.

Areas of work

  • Learning and capability development Design of practical learning solutions aligned to operational needs and workforce capability.
  • Knowledge-sharing and digital platforms Development of systems and approaches that help busy teams access, share and apply knowledge more effectively.
  • Professional learning in the flow of work Use of technology to embed development directly into day-to-day practice rather than treating learning as a separate event.
  • Consultancy and project support Support for organisations designing, reviewing or implementing learning, improvement and innovation projects.
